1. Creating a Year slicer visual card



- Add a Slicer to the Report

   - Go to the report canvas where you want to add the slicer.

   - In the `Visualizations` pane, select the `Slicer` visual.

- Drag the Year Column to the Slicer:

   - In the `Fields` pane, expand your `Calendar` table.

   - Drag the `Year` column to the `Values` field of the Slicer visual.

- Format the Slicer

   - Click on the slicer to select it.

   - Go to the `Format` pane (paint roller icon).

   - Customize the appearance of the slicer, such as font size, color, background with below formatting details:
